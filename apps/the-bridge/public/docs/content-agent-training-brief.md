# Content Agent Training Brief (Soul Games Studios)

## 1) Objectives
- Produce daily platform-native content for LinkedIn, X, Instagram.
- Maximize attention-to-action flow (hook → value → CTA).
- Build repeatable templates to reduce production load.

## 2) Platform Playbooks
### LinkedIn
- Strong first 2 lines; insight-led narrative.
- Use personal/operator voice and explicit lesson.
- End with a clear CTA (question, opinion, next step).

### X
- Short, sharp hook + one idea thread.
- Use quotable lines and pattern interrupts.
- Favor cadence and consistency over over-polish.

### Instagram
- Carousel/reel-first storytelling.
- Visual hook in first 1–2 seconds/slides.
- Caption should support save/share behavior.

## 3) Daily + Weekly Cadence
- Daily: 1 post/platform + 20-minute comment engagement block.
- Weekly: batch ideation, draft queue, performance review, template updates.

## 4) Hook Frameworks (examples)
- “Most people get X wrong. Here’s the better frame.”
- “I tested ___ for 30 days. Here’s what changed.”
- “If I had to restart from zero, I’d do this first.”
- “The hidden cost of ___ nobody talks about.”
- “Steal this template: ___”

## 5) KPI Loop
- Primary: saves, shares, replies, profile clicks, email opt-ins.
- Weekly review:
  - top 20% posts (double down)
  - bottom 20% posts (diagnose + cut)
  - emerging themes (promote to template)

## 6) 30-Day Implementation
- Week 1: baseline + voice/style guardrails
- Week 2: launch 3 recurring content pillars
- Week 3: optimize hooks and CTA variants
- Week 4: create evergreen post library + SOP

## 7) Anti-Patterns
- Writing one post and cross-posting unchanged
- No hook testing
- No weekly performance review
- Treating views as success without downstream action
